Tutorial

STEP ONE
Ensure you capture a clear image of the affected
area of your plant against a neutral,
preferably white, background.
STEP TWO
Our system analyzes one image at a time,
so make sure you upload only the best snapshot
that accurately represents the problem.
STEP THREE
After preparing your photo,
select "Upload Image" to upload
it to our classifier.
